# HelpDesk Product Marketing Brief

## Executive Summary

**Product Name**: HelpDesk (Powered by anglinAI)
**Category**: AI-Powered Help Desk & Customer Support Platform
**Target Market**: Mid-market & SMB-to-Enterprise Bridge (50-500 employees)
**Key Positioning**: "Enterprise-grade AI helpdesk at SMB pricing"

## Product Overview

HelpDesk is a comprehensive, AI-first customer support platform that bridges the gap between simple ticketing systems and complex enterprise solutions. Built with modern React/Firebase architecture, it offers enterprise-level features with the simplicity and affordability that growing businesses need.

## Core Value Proposition

**"The first AI-powered helpdesk that scales with your business without breaking your budget."**

- **60% more affordable** than enterprise solutions like Zendesk
- **AI-powered automation** that handles 80% of routine queries
- **True multi-tenant architecture** for agencies and MSPs
- **Mobile-first PWA** for modern, distributed teams
- **All-in-one platform** reducing vendor complexity
